=== BLIND REVIEW INSTRUCTIONS FOR EXTERNAL VERIFICATION ===
Phase 75.2 — Blind Review Setup

IMPORTANT: These instructions are to be given verbatim to all reviewers.
No additional explanations, no live Q&A, no founder guidance.

---

📋 WHAT YOU WILL RECEIVE:

1. External Review Packet (pre-generated match artifacts)
2. System-generated explanations for rankings
3. This instruction sheet

🎯 YOUR TASK:

You will review a ranking/match decision made by our system. Your job is to:

1. Examine the provided materials independently
2. Form your own conclusions about why the system made its choices
3. Answer the questions in the response form (to be provided separately)

⚠️ CRITICAL RULES:

DO NOT:
- Ask "why" questions live to the founder or team
- Request verbal explanations of the system
- Ask to see alternative rankings or "what-if" scenarios
- Seek reassurance about your understanding

DO:
- Work completely independently
- Note any confusion or uncertainty
- Base your assessment solely on the provided materials
- Trust your own interpretation

📝 REVIEW PROCESS:

1. Review all provided match artifacts (approx. 10-15 minutes)
2. Read the system-generated explanations
3. Form your independent assessment
4. Complete the written response form

🕐 TIMING:

- Total expected time: 20-30 minutes
- Complete in one sitting without breaks
- Submit responses immediately after completion

❓ IF YOU ARE CONFUSED:

Document your confusion in the response form. Do not:
- Ask for clarification
- Look for additional information
- Make assumptions beyond what's provided

Your experience of confusion is valuable data for us.

✅ COMPLETION:

When finished, return:
1. Completed response form
2. Any notes you took during review
3. This instruction sheet (with any margin notes you made)

---

CONFIDENTIALITY: This review is confidential. Do not discuss with other reviewers.
INTEGRITY: Your honest, unguided assessment is crucial to this process.

END OF INSTRUCTIONS
